# Rajarshi Giri

I am a 4th-year BS-MS undergraduate majoring in Physics at <a href="https://www.iiserkol.ac.in/">**IISER Kolkata**</a>. <br>
My broader interests lie in equilibrium and nonequilibrium statistical mechanics and quantum many-body physics.
However, currently, I am working on **nonequilibrium quantum dynamics in interacting many-body systems**, especially in the presence of disorder, driving, and dissipation.
